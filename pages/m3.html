Personas

(Milestone 3)

Developing Personas

Together, team members followed the process outlined by Alan Cooper for developing personas. From our affinity diagram produced in milestone two, participants were mapped along axes representing core functionality, desires, and dislikes. We were able to discern primary and secondary personas we believe generally represent our user base, as well as an anti-persona to help prevent us from getting lost in details about people outside the target user base.
