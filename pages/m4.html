Lo-Fi Prototype

(Milestone 4)

Lo-Fi Prototype Process

Team members met and discussed what set of features needed to be included in a design, but not how to implement them. Members were given a few days to come up with their own lo-fi designs for the application, implementing the key features as they saw fit. Once done, the team met again and showcased what they had created. Discussion was had surrounding the designs, both good and bad. The team selected things they liked from the different designs and combined them into the lo-fi prototype shown on this page.
